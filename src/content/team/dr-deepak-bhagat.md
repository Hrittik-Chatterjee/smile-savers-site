Dr. Deepak Bhagat founded Smile Savers in 1987 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional dental care with a personal touch. For over 35 years, he has been serving the Woodside community with dedication and expertise.

## Professional Excellence

Dr. Bhagat earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from New York University in 1987. His commitment to advancing his skills led him to achieve Diplomate status with the International Congress of Oral Implantologists (ICOI) in 2004 - a distinction held by only a select group of implant dentists worldwide.

## Philosophy of Care

"Every patient deserves to understand their treatment options and feel confident in their care," says Dr. Bhagat. His approach combines technical excellence with genuine compassion, ensuring patients feel heard and valued at every visit.

## Continuing Education

Dr. Bhagat maintains an unwavering commitment to staying current with the latest advances in dental technology and techniques. He regularly participates in continuing education courses and professional conferences to bring the best possible care to his patients.
