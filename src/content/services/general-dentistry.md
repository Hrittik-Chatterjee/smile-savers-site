At Smile Savers, our general dentistry services form the foundation of your oral health journey. Dr. Bhagat and our team provide comprehensive care designed to keep your smile healthy for life.

## What We Offer

Our general dentistry services include:

- **Comprehensive Dental Exams** - Thorough evaluation of your teeth, gums, and oral tissues
- **Professional Cleanings** - Removal of plaque and tartar buildup
- **Digital X-Rays** - State-of-the-art imaging with 90% less radiation
- **Tooth-Colored Fillings** - Natural-looking restorations for cavities
- **Fluoride Treatments** - Strengthening enamel to prevent decay
- **Oral Cancer Screenings** - Early detection for better outcomes

## Our Approach

We believe in preventive care first. By catching problems early, we can often provide simpler, more affordable treatments. Our gentle approach ensures that patients of all ages feel comfortable during their visits.
